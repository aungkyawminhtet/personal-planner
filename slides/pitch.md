# 🎯 Personal Planner
### Your AI-Powered Study Mentor

Define a goal. Get a roadmap. Stay on track.

---

# 😩 The Problem

- Learning something new is overwhelming
- People abandon goals because they don't know where to start
- No one checks in on your progress or adjusts your plan

---

# 💡 The Solution

An AI planner that:
- **Generates** a step-by-step roadmap from your goal
- **Tracks** your task completion and deadlines
- **Mentors** you with context-aware AI chat
- **Adapts** by rescheduling overdue tasks automatically

---

# 🏗️ Architecture

**Frontend:** Next.js 16 · React 19 · Tailwind CSS 4 · Zustand

**Backend:** Express 5 · MongoDB · Mongoose · JWT Auth

**AI:** Google Gemini (`gemini-3.5-flash`) for plan generation, mentor chat, and pacing analysis

---

# ✨ Key Features

- 🤖 **AI Plan Generation** — describe a goal, get a full roadmap
- 💬 **AI Mentor Chat** — ask questions, get guidance based on your project
- 📊 **Dashboard** — analytics, overdue alerts, daily missions
- 🔄 **Adaptive Pacing** — AI redistributes deadlines when you fall behind
- 📝 **Task Notes & Difficulty Reduction** — annotate tasks, simplify hard ones

---
